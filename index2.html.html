Noli Me Tangere Characters
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra Magsalin
  • A young wealthy mestizo who has recently returned to the Philippines from seven years of study in Europe.
  • Smart, well-respected and very idealistic.
Maria Clara
  • Woman of high Social Status and is believed to be Padre Damaso's goddaughter as well as the daughter of Kapitan Tiago
  • Product of a scandalous relationship between Padre Damaso and Kapitan Tiago's wife, Pia Alba
Padre Damaso
  • Elderly, Corrupt Spanish priest who has spent almost 20 years residing amongst the native Filipinos
  • prejudiced, spiteful and vengeful and he has no problem using his power and influence to destroy people who have wronged him
