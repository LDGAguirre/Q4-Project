Best Places To Go To In The Philippines

The Philippines is home to many natural resources and beautiful places, ranging from the mountains to the sandy beaches. If you are planning to go to the Philippines, here are 3 recommended places to go to.

Boracay

  • This is quite a popular tourist destination in the philippines because of the beach's crystal clear waters and white sand.
  • Boracay also offers a ton of leisurely activities such as parasailing, snorkling, kayaking, and a lot more.

Vigan

  • If you are more into historical places, Vigan is just the perfect place to go! There, you can find houses during the time of the Spanish Colonial Period, which gives you a glimpse of what it looked like during that time.

  • Aside from that, you can also ride the Kalesa which is a cart drawn by a horse.

Chocolate Hills

  • Located in Bohol, the Chocolate Hills are covered in green grass, but when it turns into summer, the grass turns brown, making the hills look like chocolate, hence the name.
